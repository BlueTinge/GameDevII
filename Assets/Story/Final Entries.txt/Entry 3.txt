16 September 1601

This afternoon, a gust of wind blew through the royal procession as it made its way across the drawbridge, causing the king's crown to tumble down into the moat below.  His majesty dived in after it, much to the shock of myself and the royal guard.  But nearly ten minutes later, he walked up from the waterbank, crown in hand, as though nothing had transpired.
His behaviour was beyond strange.  And the distant look in his eyes as he passed me by...

-Iustum Maniver
