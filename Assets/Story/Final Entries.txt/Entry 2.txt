11 August 1599

As expected, his majesty was able to deal with the hydra that had been prowling about our capital's southern border effortlessly!  He returned to the capital a hero, and the people have given him much praise.
I must say, I was skeptical when his majesty first came to me with his immortality spell.  But two years have passed since them and I have seen nothing but good things result.  Once again, his majesty has made the impossible possible!

-Iustum Maniver
