12 January 1610

Once we are made immortal, our spirits will be tied to the magical vats which His Majesty has placed around the Occultarrim.  If we are struck down, our souls will be drawn back into them, such that His Majesty may resurrect us in the future.
The very idea of living forever, defending this castle in the name of the King Talem Prodigium...I can hardly contain my excitement!

-Iustum Maniver
