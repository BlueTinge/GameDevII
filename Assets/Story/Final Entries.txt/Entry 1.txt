13 May 1597

Although our trade deal with the Genesterian council was highly successful, his majesty seems troubled.  Ever since the untimely death of his brother, he has spent more and more time in his private labs.  With his magical prowess, I wonder what could he be working on?
Well, King Talem Prodigium has only ever done what is best for Veterria.  I have complete faith that, whatever it is that he is building, it will bring our country prosperity and happiness. 

-Iustum Maniver
